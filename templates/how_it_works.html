How LabourLink Works

{# Section for Workers #}

For Workers

1

Register Once

Sign up with your name, location, skills, experience, and phone number. This is a one-time process.

2

Give a Missed Call Daily

When you are available for work, simply give a missed call to our dedicated number. This marks you as available for contractors to see.

3

Get Notified

If a contractor is interested in hiring you, you will receive an SMS notification with details on how to connect.

{# Section for Contractors #}

For Contractors

1

Login to Dashboard

Log in to your secure contractor dashboard.

2

View Available Workers

See a list of daily wage laborers who are currently available for work. Filter by skills, location, and experience.

3

Hire with a Click

Click the "Hire" button next to a worker's profile to send them an instant SMS notification about the potential job.
